    About Me

    Hi there! I'm Zaafir Ali, but everyone calls me Zaaf. I was born and raised in New Westminster, British Columbia, and soccer has been a significant part of my life since I was 4, coached by my Dad. Besides soccer, I enjoy playing basketball, badminton, volleyball, and video games like Call of Duty, FIFA, and Minecraft.

    My upbringing was deeply influenced by my parents who enrolled me in classes at a local Masjid to learn about Islam, something I now deeply appreciate. Helping the elderly during Ramadan became a cherished activity.

Now, I'm actively seeking a developer/engineer position in the tech industry while working on personal projects.
